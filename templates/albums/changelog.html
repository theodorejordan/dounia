Changelog

v0.4.3

May 17, 2026

Features

  • My submissions is now a standalone page, accessible from the sidebar

Styling

  • "Dounia" title uses the Retropix font
  • Profile page simplified: no tab navigation, content shown directly
  • Removed the delete account option
  • Log out button moved to the bottom of the profile page

v0.4.2

May 15, 2026

Features

  • Unified filter bar: single input to filter by artist, contributor, or tags
  • Album count displayed next to Collection in sidebar
  • Clickable artist names on album cards to filter by artist
  • Clickable contributor names on album cards to filter by contributor
  • Color-coded filter chips (blue for tags, green for artists, amber for contributors)

v0.4.1

May 14, 2026

Features

  • Album cards now show the user who submitted them (avatar + username)
  • Pending submissions count badge in sidebar and profile nav
  • Albums require at least one tag before submission

Fixes

  • Tags now correctly saved when admin adds an album directly

v0.4.0

May 13, 2026

Features

  • Registered users can now submit albums for review
  • Track your submissions and their status in your profile
  • Redesigned profile page with sidebar navigation

Fixes

  • Profile now has separate pages for account info and submissions
  • Logout now asks for confirmation
